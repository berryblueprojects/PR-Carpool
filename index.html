About Us.

PR Carpool App is intended to share the car space with others called as co-travellers who are willing to travel the same way around thereby the ride expenses can be shared. Car owners and ride takers can meet at a mentioned time and commute to and from the same locations. Online booking and car owners details are ready just a few clicks away. Call/Message or online book and start a happy ride with them. Join hands to save fuels and save nature. Start sharing and riding.

Privacy and data protection Policy.

PR Carpool collects basic information during registration with the app like name, date of birth, gender, email mobile number etc which are considered as the profile information of the user(member of the PR Car app). The profile information are public in the PR Carpool's app scope and hence one user may view the profile of another and see the profile details. The restriction of the profile informations to other users are currently public and may be customizable in coming time. The rides those are booked by a user are only viewed by the user who booked and the user who had posted the ride. The rides those are posted by a user are public and can be viewed by anyone who logged into the app. The details of the users who have booked a particular ride can be seen by the user who had posted the ride. We may collect and store other information such as network information, your location details or any other details which you enter or type inside the PR Carpool app at any point of time. We may use this information to improve our technicalities and user experience. We may share users information with the government body or courts of India if demanded by them under the law. We may share your information with third party bodies for joint development and collaboration or maybe promotion purpose as well.
